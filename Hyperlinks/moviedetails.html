Movie details

- Shershaah is a 2021 Hindi-language Indian biographical war film.
- It is about the life of Captain Vikram Batra, an Indian soldier who was killed in action during the 1999 Kargil War between India and Pakistan.
- Batra was posthumously awarded the Param Vir Chakra, India's highest military honor, for his bravery in helping India win the war.
